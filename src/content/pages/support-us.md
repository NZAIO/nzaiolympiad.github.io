The New Zealand AI Olympiad (NZAIO) is a volunteer-led, non-profit initiative building opportunities for students to learn advanced AI problem-solving and represent New Zealand internationally. We welcome support from individuals, schools, universities, and organisations that want to help students access training, mentoring, and travel opportunities.

## How people can help

- Sponsor training activities, camps, or student resources
- Support travel and accommodation for international events
- Offer mentoring, partnerships, or venue support
- Help us connect with students across New Zealand

## Founding support

We gratefully acknowledge support from:

- [TAIAO](https://www.taiao.net/)
- [Science Olympiad New Zealand (SONZ)](https://scienceolympianz.org.nz/)
